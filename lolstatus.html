LOL Parser

Check the service status of your League of Legends server.

Find LOL Status on the Google Play Store.

  • Server status for nine League of Legends servers, including NA and EUW.
  • Keep tabs on multiple servers at once using the home page.
  • See which services are affected, and read status updates from Riot.
  • Easily refresh to get new updates.
  • Select which servers you want to see on the home page in settings.

Upcoming features:

  • Animated home page.
  • Tablet-specific layout.
