Who are we? What do we do?

Established in 1988 as a specialty store for imported vinyl records, DMR has made an impact on the Japanese music retail industry.

Over the past 25 years, this entrepreneurial business has adapted continuously to the changing economic environment. In 2011, it switched its core business from records to music equipment.

DMR continues to evolve not only as a distributor of premium audio brands, but also as a provider of innovative web services and mobile applications.

As an intern at DMR, you will gain experience in all aspects of business and see firsthand how a small, entrepreneurial company can thrive in a competitive industry.
