Who are we? What do we do?

Established in 1988 as a specialty store for imported vinyl records, DMR has made a presence in the Japanese music retail industry.

Within the past 25 years, this entrepreneurial business has adapted to the ever-changing situation and in 2011, switched its core merchandise from records to music equipment.

Now, DMR continues to evolve not only as a distributor of premium audio brands, but also as a provider of innovative web services and mobile applications.
